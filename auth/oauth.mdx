# I think that is going to be tricky...

<Warning>
    Apple is easy, Google hard.
</Warning>

#### Thats because of the Google's OAuth SHA1 fingerprint.

<Tip>
    You can generate a SHA1 fingerprint for your app [here](https://developers.google.com/android/guides/client-auth).
</Tip>
